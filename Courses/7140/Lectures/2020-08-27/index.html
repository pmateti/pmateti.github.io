2.2 Programming in the Small .. Giga

  1. Can we define: program, software? Small, Medium, Large, …, Giga?
  2. Our working/arbirary definitions:
    1. Tiny: 00 – 01 KLOC;
    2. Small: 01 – 10 KLOC;
    3. Medium: 10 – 01 MLOC;
    4. Large: 01 – 10 MLOC;
    5. Giga: 10+ MLOC
