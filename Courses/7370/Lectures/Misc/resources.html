OSIS Lab

This is the lab associated with the course.  The machines in the lab are setup to boot into Linux or Windows XP. You are expected to work on the projects using a Linux system.  You are expected to turnin the files of your projects using the turnin program accessible in this lab.

It is ok to reboot the machines, as needed.  But, please do not turn the machines off without a proper shut down.  Please read the OSIS Lab Policies.
